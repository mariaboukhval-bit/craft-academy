POC · Ryan Zurowski

Seven consolidated themes shaping the strain

  1. Internal workload growth has outpaced operating model evolution
  2. Structural misalignment creates ownership ambiguity
  3. Governance is fragmented across guidelines and intake
  4. Squad rigidity reduces system flexibility
  5. No single cross-marketing trade-off authority
  6. Hidden operational load reduces effective capacity
  7. Escalations and reactive prioritization have become normalized

Three critical decision points

1
Governance & Standards Consolidation
Centralize ownership of creative standards and intake for all creative work.
2
Capacity Management & Workload Elasticity
Redefine the allocation model to increase flexibility and ring-fence strategic capacity.
3
Clear Ownership & Escalation Authority
Establish a clear escalation pathway and singular decision-maker for cross-functional creative trade-offs.
